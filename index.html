About

JDStudios was created for the purpose of providing high-quality, virtual lessons to students of all ages, backgrounds, and aspirations. Each lesson is designed to fit the student’s needs and goals and offered by skilled teachers who know their craft. In addition to training and extensive performance experience, our instructors have a combined 30 years of teaching experience. You can learn more about our staff in their bios below.

Whether you are seeking to explore a creative outlet, prepare for college auditions, brush up some old skills or learn some new ones, JDStudios is ready to help! We are dedicated to your success and look forward to working with you!

Voice

Dianna Hollowell

Hello! My name is Dianna. I have a Bachelor of Music in Vocal Performance from Biola University and earned my Master of Music in Musical Theater from Oklahoma City University. In addition to performing in both musicals and operas, I’ve enjoyed teaching one-on-one voice lessons, leading group voice, piano, and musical theater classes, and working as a music director and vocal coach for numerous adult and children's theatre productions.

Since I began teaching in 2008, I've had the privilege of working with hundreds of students with widely differing skill levels and musical backgrounds and I am passionate about helping each of my students reach their personal goals, learn healthy vocal technique, and gain confidence in their singing abilities. I look forward to talking with you about your musical background, interests, and goals.

Tap

Laurie Deards Chase

Hi, I’m Laurie! Growing up in a community theatre environment, I was beyond excited to get my first pair of tap shoes when I was six years old – and I’ve had a pair within reach ever since! My earliest teaching experience was assisting with instruction for beginning and intermediate tappers. While earning a degree at Cal Poly Pomona (B.A. in English, Education Emphasis), I taught performance skills to young people in elementary, middle, and high school settings. As a choreographer for local musical theatre productions, I’ve now been able to dance with people of all ages.

I love teaching movement skills to performers. I’m dedicated to seeing each learner as an individual, helping them to succeed and feel their best while dancing. We work together to build safe, sustainable habits and find joy in movement!

Acting/ General Manager

Acting/ General Manager

Jeff Deards Jr.

Welcome to JD Studios! I'm Jeff. Having grown up in the So Cal community theatre scene, I attended California State University Fullerton where I graduated with a BFA in Musical Theatre and spent the next several years performing professionally on both coasts. Along with my on-stage theatrical work, I've had the opportunity to appear as a featured vocalist for the Florida Orchestra's Holiday Pops concert, as well as perform in Europe where I recorded with the London Symphony Orchestra at Abbey Road Studios.

In addition to performing, I have had the privilege of spending the last several years teaching, directing, and producing both youth and adult theatre. I particularly love working with students and actors to help build their skills, gain confidence on stage and tell clear, captivating, stories. As the General Manager of JDStudios, I'm here to assist you so please feel free to reach out at any time!
