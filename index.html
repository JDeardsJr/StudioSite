About

Voice

Welcome to JD Studios! My name is Dianna. I have a Bachelor of Music in Vocal Performance from Biola University and earned my Master of Music in Musical Theater from Oklahoma City University. Since I began teaching in 2008, I’ve enjoyed teaching one-on-one voice lessons, leading group voice, piano, and musical theater classes, and working as a music director and vocal coach for numerous adult and children's theatre productions.

I've had the privilege of working with hundreds of students with widely differing skill levels and musical backgrounds and I am passionate about helping each of my students reach their personal goals, learn healthy vocal technique, and gain confidence in their singing abilities. I look forward to talking with you about your musical background, interests, and goals.
