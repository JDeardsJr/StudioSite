This 6 week interactive class will consist of topic discussions, demonstrations, vocal exercises and warm-ups as well as in-class performance opportunities for students who (voluntarily, of course) wish to receive personalized feedback and guidance.

In this online class you will learn:

  • How your voice works and how to use it healthfully
  • The fundamentals of singing techique
  • How to develop your vocal sound
  • Specific excercises to strengthen your voice and build healthy vocal technique

Join Dianna in a safe, supportive environment where you will learn the building blocks of singing, explore your voice, and gain confidence in your singing abilities. If you have a healthy speaking voice, you can learn to sing!
