Lesson Payment Information

JDStudios currently offers two payment methods:

To Pay With Venmo:

  • Scan the QR code located above or in the upper left hand corner of your Invoice*Scan the QR code located to the right or in the upper left hand corner of your Invoice*
    (You can also find us in the Venmo App by searching JD-Studios)
  • Input payment as specified on Invoice*
  • Under "What's it for?", please list the student's name and Invoice#**

*Invoice can be found by viewing your Invoice Email's attachments

**Invoice# is located in upper right hand corner of Invoice

To Pay With Credit/Debit/PayPal:

  • Click the “Click Here to Pay Online” button on your Invoice*
  • If you do not have a PayPal account, simply click “Checkout as Guest” and provide an email address
  • Click “Continue to Payment” and proceed with your credit/debit card payment

*Invoice can be found by viewing your Invoice Email's attachments

Important:

Tuition for lessons is paid by the month or the session. All lessons are pre-paid. Lesson times are not reserved until payment is received. After scheduling a lesson, the time slot may be held for 24 hours in order to provide time for the student to submit payment.

However, students are encouraged to submit payment immediately in order to reserve their time. After 24 hours, the time slot will be released to other students to book.

Questions or concerns?

Please speak to our General Manager if you have any questions or need to discuss alternate payment options.
